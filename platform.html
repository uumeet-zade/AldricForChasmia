The Anti-Big Corp Platform

We are building an economy that rewards those who build it - by hand and by brain. Not the monopolies that exploit us.

Real Anti-Monopoly Protection

We will enact strict anti-monopoly laws to prevent retail chains from undercutting local businesses with predatory pricing. This isn't a subsidy that can be cut, it's a structural rule that ensures a fair playing field for everyone.

Tax Relief for Small Enterprises

We promise tax relief specifically targeting small businesses and market stalls. Instead of blanket cuts that only help big players, our plan ensures the backbone of our community gets the support it needs to thrive and grow.

High-Speed Rail for Rural Nodes

Our infrastructure plan connects rural communities directly to the wider network. Towns like Littlewood will become vital nodes, allowing local producers to reach city markets in hours, without abandoning their roots.

Empowering Local Producers

By providing the infrastructure and fair rules, small vendors and local artisans will have the reach big chains already possess. Growth shouldn't mean leaving your town; it should mean thriving within it.

Union Labor Guarantee on Rail Infrastructure

All High-Speed Rail construction and operations jobs prioritize unionized labor. This turns the connectivity plan into a direct jobs program for organized workers alongside expanding our transit network.

Stronger Collective Bargaining Protections

We will establish legal safeguards preventing employers from bypassing or dissolving unions, with real enforcement behind the right to organize and strike.
